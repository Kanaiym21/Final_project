I watched a video lesson and wrote every word and documented it, so sign language translators could translate those video lessons for students with disabilities. Then I decided to create one platform for both students and teachers across the country to conduct and participate in lessons. It would make it easier for students with disabilities to continue to learn despite any circumstances. It was so meaningful to me because I could make my contribution to getting an education through better technologies. Then I chose computer science.
